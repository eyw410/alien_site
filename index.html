Motivation: People often think of aliens as scary beings with bad intentions. I wanted to subvert that notion and invent inhabitants of a "faraway planet" who are ridiculously happy. Unfortunately, I think that a planet filled with joy and playfulness would be considered quite strange and unfamiliar to us.

So, I drew these aliens on PowerPoint and made these gifs (using https://icons8.com/animizer/en/animate-static-image and https://gifmaker.me/). Sure, having unlocked the secrets of happiness and peace, these creatures might be light-years smarter than us and have the ability to process and enjoy sensory input much faster than we do, but they still make sure we can share in their happiness! :)

This music was produced with GarageBand's piano that you can play using your computer keyboard. The song has a theme and variations form, with a human-speed theme and alien-speed variations. Furthermore, there is a theme with melodic variations within each part.
